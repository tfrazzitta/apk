Another Solution if your Firestick is running slow


  • Step 1: Go To Settings

  • Step 2: From Settings, click on Applications

  • Step 3: In Applications, Click on Manage Installed Applications

  • Step 4: Scroll down to Cinema HD and click on it

  • Step 5: Scroll down and click on clear data and clear it

  • Step 6: Go back and clear cache

  • Step 7: Repeat that process for all the apps you frequently use
